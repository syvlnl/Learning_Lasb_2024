Meet the Munchkin Cat

The Adorable Short-Legged Cat

The Munchkin cat is known for its short legs, playful personality, and affectionate nature. They are friendly, sociable cats that enjoy being around people and other pets. Despite their short stature, Munchkins are agile and love to play.
